NPL Football Rules

  • The referee’s decision will be final and binding.
  • If the match ends in a draw, a penalty shoot-out will be held (5 kicks per team).
  • If still tied, penalty kicks will continue until one team wins.
  • During elimination rounds, the team with the lowest points will be eliminated.
  • Each win awards 1 point.
  • In case of injury, a player may be replaced by one of equal or lower skill level, with approval from other captains.
  • Elimination Match Duration --> 35 minutes (2 halves of 15 minutes each), with a 5-minute break.
  • After 15 minutes of play, both teams must switch goalposts.
  • Team Size → 5 players (including goalkeeper). Each team must have at least 4 players to start a match.
  • The offside rule will be there. If the opponent secures a goal while inside or touching the small box (present near to goal post) and there are no team players present inside the box, then the goal won't be counted.
  • Cornet kick will be there.
  • Ball In & Out of Play: The ball is in play as long as it has not fully crossed the touchline or goal line, whether on the ground or in the air. The ball is out of play when it has completely crossed the line.
  • If the ball goes out of play on the sideline, restart with a throw over the head. A throw-in must be delivered from behind the boundary line and over the head with both feet on the ground. If not thrown properly, possession goes to the opponent.
  • Free Kick Rules → Opponents must stand at least 1 meter away during free kicks, corners, and kick-ins. Self-start is not allowed
  • Match Start Signal: Players can start active play only after 3 drops of the football by the referee.
  • The starting kick or throw (from the goal post) should be done by goal keeper only. Self-start is not allowed.
  • Fouls & Misconduct: Pushing, holding, tripping, or deliberate handball results in a penalty or free kick (after 3 penalties in a single match).
  • If, during a match, the ball touches any player’s hand or arm (below the shoulder), whether intentional or unintentional, a free kick will be awarded to the opposing team. A penalty kick will be awarded if the incident occurs inside the penalty box.
  • Referee Authority: The referee has full control of the match, and their decisions are final. Players must respect the referee and follow their instructions.
  • Note: A player who has been successfully bid cannot represent more than one team. In case a player is unavailable or gets injured during a match, they may be replaced only from the limited pool of available players (Abhijeet, Sarthak, Bhubnesh, Runal), and such replacement must have prior approval from all the captains.
  • Due to time constraints, we request everyone to be available during the scheduled time slots. Captains, please ensure that all your players are present at the designated time. If any player is unavailable, kindly inform us here in advance (before 11pm today) so that we can try to adjust the slots accordingly.
